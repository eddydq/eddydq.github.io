Embedded Hardware & Firmware

Engineering clean cadence data on the water.

Paddling Pulse is an open-source hardware project centered on the Renesas DA14531MOD. It captures real-time stroke rate using custom PCBs, embedded C firmware, and fixed-point DSP algorithms.

Specifications

Engineered for precision and power efficiency.

BLE CSCP Protocol

Broadcasts real-time paddle stroke rate as "crank cadence" using the BLE Cycling Speed & Cadence Profile (0x1816), enabling instant compatibility with Wahoo, Garmin, and Zwift.

Autonomy

Optimized power consumption utilizing the ultra-low power states of the DA14531MOD, allowing for hundreds of hours of paddling on a single coin-cell battery.

Ultra-Compact Size

Designed with an extremely small footprint to mount seamlessly on any paddle shaft without affecting weight balance or hydrodynamics.
